College of Engineering, Guindy

The College of Engineering is one of the top-most educational institutes in Tamil Nadu. Situated in Guindy , this college holds the key for drafting the syllabus for the other colleges too.The college takes up nearly 300 acres of land and has more than 11 hostel blocks.
